HappyJS

  1. Choose a HappyJS Strategy

    • Auto Validate HappyJS HTML: Design your HTML form according to HappyJS specifications and get auto validation out of the box!
    • Auto Validate CUSTOM HTML: Extend HappyJS and/or override HappyJS methods to teach HappyJS where to find and render form elements.
    • Single Field Validation: Provide a single field CSS-Selector and the HappyJS config to match.
    • Auto validation with Single Field overrides: You can combine your auto validation setup with single field overrides to cater for those not-so-often-used custom field types.
